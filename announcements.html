District Convention Launch

Cal-Nev-Ha Circle K is proud to present District Convention 2020: Sweet Dreams CNH!

As the day comes to an end, the California-Nevada-Hawai'i District prepares to go to bed. Grab your pajamas, slippers, facemasks and get ready to get some well-deserved rest! Our dream destination is the Riverside Convention Center and Marriott.

Make sure your nightcaps are on tight because you're going to go through a wholesome weekend of dreamy memories with election and preparation of our new leaders, memorizing workshops, and looking into the future with our professional exposition. Remember to also take a moment to reflect and count our achievements with all of the friends we've made throughout the day. Get ready for some sweet dreams CNH!

Be sure to check out our Facebook page and website for updates!

November 5th 2019
