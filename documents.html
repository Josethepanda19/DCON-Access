REGISTRATION DOCUMENTS (REQUIRED)

These documents are required to register your club for District Convention. Please read the registration guide before proceeding. The DCON Registration Form is to be filled out once per submission and each attendee is required to submit the "Wavier + Attendee Agreement Form".

Early registration is due on Febuary 21st and regular registration is due on Febuary 28th. Hotel Registration is due on March 5th.
